Why We Serve Local

Serving others is an opportunity to demonstrate the love of Christ to the world in a tangible way. Whether its delivering a meal, building a fence, or mentoring a student, service meets a physical need that builds bridges for spiritual conversations and transformation. Whether done in public or private, locally or globally, we serve that God may be glorified.

Types of Serving Commitments

One-Time: are non-committal, one-time opportunities. (Monthly events as well)

Ongoing: these can be weekly.
